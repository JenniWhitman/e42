Converting from decimal to hexadecimal is very easy as long as you can divide by 16.

1. Take the decimal number and divide by 16. Put the remainder, as a hexadecimal value (meaning 10-15 need to be converted to A-F) in column A. Carry the answer to the next step.
2. Divide the answer from above (without remainder) by 16. The next remainder (in hex) goes in column B. The answer is carried on.
3. Continue this until you can divide by 16 no further. Put all of these numbers together and you have an answer!
